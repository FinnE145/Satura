Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites function or to remember your preferences across visits.

2. How Satura Uses Cookies

Satura uses only essential session cookies. These are set by our authentication system when you log in, and are used solely to keep you signed in. No other cookies are set by Satura.

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond maintaining your login session.

3. Cookie Details

Name Purpose Type Duration
session Maintains your authenticated session Essential 7 days from login

4. Third-Party Cookies

Satura does not use any third-party cookies. No external services, advertisers, or analytics platforms set cookies through our site.

5. Your Choices

You can instruct your browser to refuse or delete cookies at any time. Note that disabling cookies will prevent you from logging in, as the session cookie is required for authentication to function.

6. Legal Basis for Use

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of Satura. Under applicable privacy laws — including Canada's PIPEDA, the EU's GDPR, and US state privacy frameworks — strictly necessary cookies do not require your prior consent. By choosing to use Satura, you acknowledge the use of these cookies as described above.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised.
